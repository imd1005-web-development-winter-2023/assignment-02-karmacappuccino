OUR PHILOSOPHY

At first impression, we're no different from your average salon: buzzing with blow dryers and conversations driven by coffee. Yet, we like to take a more leisurely approach and slow things down. We pay attention to our clients, remember the names of their children just as we do with our friends, and never double-book. We exclusively purchase non-toxic hair products, and 95% of our waste is recycled. In essence, we created a salon experience that is both opulent and environmentally friendly so that you can have your cake and eat it too. Welcome to Luxe Hair and Salon
